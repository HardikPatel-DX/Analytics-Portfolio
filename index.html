How to Use the Margin Erosion Tracker

Prepare your CSV with the columns below, upload it, and get instant margin analysis.

Required Columns (must be present)

ColumnAccepted Header NamesNotes
Date / Period REQ date, period, month, year, quarter, week YYYY-MM, MM/YYYY, Q1-2024, or full dates
Product / SKU REQ product, sku, item, description, name, part Any text identifier per line
Revenue / Sales REQ revenue, sales, net sales, gross sales Dollar amount per row (e.g. 12500 or $12,500)
Cost / COGS REQ cost, cogs, cost of goods, total cost, direct cost Dollar amount per row — must be < revenue for positive margin

Optional Columns (unlock richer insights)

ColumnAccepted Header NamesUnlocks
Category OPT category, segment, family, group, class, dept Erosion grouped by segment
Volume / Units OPT units, volume, qty, quantity, pieces Mix-shift vs. rate analysis
Unit Price OPT price, unit price, selling price, list price, asp Price-driven vs. cost-driven root cause
